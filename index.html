// Biography

Hi, I’m Md. Samiul Ajom, a passionate and skilled Front-End Web Developer with a strong background in creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly web interfaces. I specialize in HTML5, CSS3 (including Bootstrap and Tailwind),SASS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like ReactJS and Next.js. With a keen eye for design and functionality, I aim to deliver seamless and innovative web experience.

I am currently pursuing a Diploma in Computer Technology at Chapainawabganj Polytechnic Institute, where I continue to hone my technical skills and knowledge. My dedication to delivering quality work extends to my practical experience, including a 6-month workshop at NSSTHEME, where I contributed to real-world projects. Beyond my professional journey, I take pride in my strong problem-solving abilities and my commitment to staying updated with the latest web development trends. Whether it's collaborating with teams or working independently, I thrive on building solutions that leave a lasting impression.
